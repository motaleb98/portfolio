Somapura Mahavihara

Somapura Mahavihara (Bengali: সোমপুর মহাবিহার, romanized: Shompur Môhabihar) in Paharpur, Badalgachhi Upazila, Naogaon District, Bangladesh is among the best known viharas, centres of learning, in the Indian Subcontinent and is one of the most important archaeological sites in the country.
